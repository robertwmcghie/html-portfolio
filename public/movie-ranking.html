The Best Movies According To Robbie

My top 5 movies of all -time

The Godfather (1972, 1974, 1990)

Widely regarded as one of the greatest films of all time, this three-part mob drama, based on Mario's Puzo's novel of the same name
focuses on the powerful Italian-American crime family of Don Vito Corleone (Marlon Brandon). When his youngest son Michael
(Al Pacino), reluctantly joins the mafia, he becomes involved in the inevitable cycle of violence and betrayal. Although Michael tries
to maintain a normal relationship with his wife Kay (Diane Keaton) , he is drawn deeper into the family business.

The Towering Inferno (1974)

Sporting an all-star cast, this 1970s movie is about a high rise state-of-the-art luxury apartment/office complex in San Francisco,
that went up in flames during the opening night ceremony attended by a host of A-list guests, due to cheap and faulty electrical
wireing. An overworked fire chief (Steve McQueen) and the building's architect (Paul Newman), must cooperate in the struggle to
save lives and subdue panic, while a corrupt cost-cutting contractor tried to evasder responsibility for the disaster.

The Gangster Wars (1981)

This 1981 film tells the story of three teenagers, based on real life gangsters Charles "Lucky" Luciano (Michael Nouri), Benjamin
"Bugsy" Siegel (Joe Penny), and Michael Lasker (Brian Benben) (a fictional character modeled after real life gangster Meyer Lansky),
growing up in New York's ghettos during the earfly 1900s to their rise through organized crime in the prohibition era.

Hands Of A Stranger (1987)

A New York cop, Joe Hearn (Armand Assante) becomes obsessed with finding his wife's rapist, even without her wanting him to, for fear he
will find out she had gone to the scene of the crime to cheat with the coach of her sons's high school basketball team. But when she had second
thoughts and was about to leave, the perpetrator entered to commit the heinous crime.

Fine Things (1990)

Based on the Danielle Stelle's best-selling novel of the same name, this emotionally charged drama is about a successful business owner Bernie
Fine, who thought he had found the love to last a lifetime, but when Liz is stricken with cancer shortly after the birth of their first child, time becomes
painfully short. Alone with two children, Bernie must face the loss and learn how to move on. New people, new experiences, a new life. Will he find
happiness again?
